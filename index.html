Introduction

At AAA Handyman Services, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. Information We Collect

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in these limited cases:

Our website uses essential cookies for functionality and analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand visitor trends. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. We do not use tracking for advertising purposes.

Your Rights and Choices

Contact us using the details below to exercise these rights.
