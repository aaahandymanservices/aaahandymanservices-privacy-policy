Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

📋 Introduction

At AAA Handyman Services, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website aaahandyman.services, contact us, or engage our handyman and home improvement services in the Waterford/Keego Harbor, Michigan area and surrounding communities.

We are a locally owned and operated business dedicated to reliable, honest, and professional service. Your privacy is important to us, and we handle your information with the same care we bring to every home repair, deck build, or maintenance project.

🔍 Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Contact details (name, phone number, email, address)
  • Service request details & project descriptions
  • Payment details processed securely
  • Communication records and project photos

Automatically Collected Information

  • Website usage details (IP, pages visited)
  • Browser and device specifications

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

  • 1 To provide, schedule, and complete requested handyman services.
  • 2 To communicate about appointments, estimates, and follow-ups.
  • 3 To process payments and send receipts.
  • 4 To improve our services and overall website experience.
  • 5 To send occasional updates about promotions or seasonal maintenance tips.

🤝 Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in these limited cases:

  • With trusted service partners or subcontractors needed to complete your project.
  • With secure payment processors and essential web analytics providers.
  • When required by law or to protect the safety and rights of our customers and team.

🍪 Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses essential cookies for functionality and basic analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand visitor trends and improve responsiveness. We do not use tracking or cookies for third-party targeted advertising.

🔒 Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no internet-based system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

⚖️ Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to request access to or correction of your personal information, ask us to delete your data (subject to legal requirements), or opt out of any marketing communications. Feel free to contact us anytime to exercise these options.

🛡️ Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or maintain data from children.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version here with an updated effective date at the top of the page.
